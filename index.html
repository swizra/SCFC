What SCFC offers

The Soyth Consulting & Finance Club (SCFC) offers FHNW students practical consulting experience while providing companies with motivated support. Students benefit from:

Real Consulting Projects

Small interdisciplinary student teams work part-time on real business challenges for several weeks. Projects span from market analysis to financial modeling to strategic planning.

Real Professional Experience

A platform enabling students to apply their business and finance skills to solve real challenges. Students gain varied insights into different industries while providing real value.

Real Community & Impact

A platform of like-minded peers interested in consulting, finance and business. Real, in-depth collaboration with multiple companies instead of just networking events.

SCFC x Companies

The Soyth Consulting & Finance Club (SCFC) enables companies to gain access to structured consulting from motivated FHNW teams. Companies benefit from:

Dedicated consulting support

Student teams work with your company on a concrete business challenge for several weeks while providing research, analysis and structured recommendations.

Diverse perspectives

Motivated FHNW students with diverse backgrounds and experiences bring complementary viewpoints to your business challenges and drive impact with minimal costs.

Access & visibility

Engagement with SCFC increases your visibility at FHNW and connects you with our students and alumni network. Many of our motivated students go on to become professionals at top firms.

Our Process

SCFC follows a structured approach to deliver high-quality consulting projects.

1. Project Scoping

We work with companies to define clear project objectives and deliverables, ensuring alignment with business needs.

2. Team Formation

Interdisciplinary student teams are carefully selected based on skills and interests to match project requirements.

3. Execution & Delivery

Teams conduct research, analysis, and provide actionable recommendations, with regular check-ins to ensure quality.

Join as a Student

We're looking for curious, motivated, and skilled students with an interest in business, finance, or consulting. Prior experience is a plus, but not required. To apply, please send your CV and a short note about your interests to our email.

Who Can Join?

All FHNW students are welcome to join. Cross-university collaborations are also encouraged, so if you are a student from another university, feel free to send us a message.

Time Commitment

Expect a commitment of around 2–3 hrs/week during project blocks. No commitment is required during exam periods.

How to Apply

Send your CV and a brief note about your interests and relevant experience to hello@soyth.ch. We look forward to hearing from you!
