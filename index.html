We believe the best way to learn is by doing, and we're building a community where ambitious students can gain real consulting experience while creating tangible value for businesses. Our values reflect what matters most to us.

Growth

Innovation

We encourage fresh thinking and aren't afraid to challenge conventional approaches. Our student perspective often brings creative solutions that established firms might overlook.

What We Offer Students

SCFC bridges the gap between classroom theory and professional practice. Whether you're from FHNW, HSG, ZHAW, ETH, or another university, you'll find meaningful opportunities to apply what you're learning while building skills that set you apart.

Real Consulting Projects

Work in small, interdisciplinary teams on actual business challenges over several weeks. Projects range from market entry strategies and competitive analysis to financial modeling and operational optimization. You'll see your recommendations implemented.

Practical Experience

This isn't another case competition. You'll interact directly with company stakeholders, conduct primary research, analyze real data, and present actionable recommendations. The experience you gain here looks impressive on any CV—because it's genuine.

Community & Network

Connect with driven students who share your ambitions. Our members come from diverse academic backgrounds and career interests, creating a network that extends across multiple universities and industries. Many alumni have gone on to top consulting firms and finance roles.

How We Work

We've developed a structured approach that ensures quality deliverables while maximizing learning opportunities for our student teams.

1. Project Scoping

We work closely with companies to define clear objectives, deliverables, and success metrics. This ensures everyone's aligned from day one and sets teams up for success.

2. Team Formation

We carefully assemble interdisciplinary teams of 3-4 students, matching skills and interests to project needs. Teams often include students from different universities, enriching the collaborative experience.

3. Execution & Delivery

Teams work autonomously with regular check-ins, conducting research, analyzing data, and developing recommendations. Projects conclude with a formal presentation and comprehensive written report.

Join SCFC

We're looking for motivated students who want more than what's offered in lecture halls. Whether you're studying business, economics, engineering, or something else entirely, if you're curious about consulting and finance, we want to hear from you.

Who Should Apply?

Students from FHNW, HSG, ZHAW, ETH, and other Swiss universities are all welcome. We value intellectual curiosity, strong work ethic, and collaborative spirit over specific experience. If you're willing to learn and contribute, apply.

Time Commitment

Expect to dedicate 2-4 hours per week during active project phases, typically lasting 6-10 weeks. We're flexible around exam periods and understand that academics come first. Most members take on 1-2 projects per semester.

Application Process

Email your CV and a brief message explaining why you're interested in SCFC and what you hope to gain from the experience. We review applications on a rolling basis and aim to respond within a week.
