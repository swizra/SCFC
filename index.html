How We Work

Our structured approach to consulting

1. Project Scoping

We work closely with companies to define clear objectives, deliverables, and success metrics. This ensures everyone's aligned from day one and sets teams up for success.

2. Team Formation

We carefully assemble interdisciplinary teams of 3-4 students, matching skills and interests to project needs. Teams often include students from different universities.

3. Execution & Delivery

Teams work autonomously with regular check-ins, conducting research, analyzing data, and developing recommendations. Projects conclude with a formal presentation and comprehensive written report.

4. Impact Measurement

We track the implementation and results of our recommendations, ensuring our work creates tangible value and learning opportunities for future projects.
